One Week Friends

Genre: Drama Romance

Cast: Haruna Kawaguchi, Kento Yamazaki, Takashi Matsuo

Synopsis: High schooler Yūki Hase notices that his classmate Kaori Fujimiya is always alone and seemingly has no friends. After approaching her and becoming better acquainted, Kaori reveals that every Monday she loses all memory of her friends. Despite learning this, Yūki endeavors to befriend her anew every week.
